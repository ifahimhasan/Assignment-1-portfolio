Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 runs September 15-17, 2026 at the San Francisco Convention Center, 747 Howard St, San Francisco, CA 94103.

Is there a student discount?

Yes! Students with a valid .edu email can apply for a 40% discount on the Starter tier. Use code STUDENT2026 at checkout. Limited spots available.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event (Aug 15). After that, tickets are transferable but non-refundable.

Are sessions recorded?

All keynotes and talks are recorded and made available to all ticket holders within 2 weeks post-event via our portal.

What does the Hackathon involve?

Day 3 is our 8-hour hackathon. Teams of 2–4 build projects around the conference theme. Prizes include cash, credits, and job interviews at sponsor companies.

Is there a Code of Conduct?

Absolutely. DevConf is committed to a harassment-free experience. All attendees, speakers, and sponsors agree to our CoC. Violations are taken seriously.
